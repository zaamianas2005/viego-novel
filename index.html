The Lore of Camavor

Explore the world behind the tragedy

The Fall of a Kingdom

"Viego: The Broken Crown" is a dark fantasy novel set in the world of League of Legends' Runeterra. It explores the tragic fall of King Viego, his descent into madness, and the catastrophic Ruination that followed.

The story begins in the shining kingdom of Camavor, where young Viego inherits a throne he never wanted. His love for Isolde, a seamstress from a distant land, becomes both his salvation and his damnation.
